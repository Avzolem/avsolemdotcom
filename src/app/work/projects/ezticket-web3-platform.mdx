## Project Overview

EzTicket revolutionizes event ticketing by leveraging blockchain technology to create secure, non-duplicable tickets as NFTs on the Solana blockchain.

## Problem Solved

Traditional ticketing systems suffer from:
- Easy duplication and fraud
- Scalping and price manipulation
- Lack of transparency
- No control over secondary markets

EzTicket solves these issues through blockchain technology.
